Former DIPP Secy Ramesh Abhishek has not applied for SEBI chief post: Govt

Former DIPP Secretary Ramesh Abhishek had not applied for the post of chairman of Securities Exchange Board of India (SEBI), the Centre said. The development comes amid speculations that he is one of the top contenders for the post of chief.



A writ had sought direction to the Central Government to ensure that a fair procedure is being adopted while not considering the case of the Ramesh Abhishek to the post of Chairman of



When the matter came up for hearing last week before the Divisional Bench, comprising of Justice MM Sundresh and Justice R Hemalatha, Senior Panel counsel for the Central Government N Ramesh informed the Bench that he (Ramesh Abhishek) has not even applied for the post of the Chairman of and therefore, there is no question of shortlisting him for the said post and thereafter considering him.
