YES Bank crisis: Paying loan? Shift ECS mandate to a nationalised bank

Coming soon after the Punjab and Maharashtra Co-operative (PMC) Bank scam, the events at YES Bank have left deposit holders a shaken lot. However, they should take heart from the fact that the regulator has acted very rapidly in this case, so their troubles may end soon.

But they, too, need to take a few steps to contain the adverse fallout of this development. The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) superseded the board of YES Bank and imposed a month-long moratorium on it.
