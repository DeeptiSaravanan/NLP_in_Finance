Interesting nudge from Sebi with takeovers

It is an extraordinary measure that has taken everyone by surprise. The Securities and Exchange Board of India (Sebi) has amended the takeover regulations to enable an additional creeping acquisition of 5 per cent of the voting rights in listed companies within this financial year without making an open offer.
