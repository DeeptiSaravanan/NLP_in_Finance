Clarity for FDI

The government some weeks ago moved to increase scrutiny of investment in India from countries with which it shares a land border. This action was introduced through a note from the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, which revised the foreign direct investment policy to curb opportunistic takeovers of Indian companies.
