RIL mega issue to put Sebi's 'rights entitlement' platform to test

Reliance Industries’ (RIL’s) mega rights issues will put to test the platform for trading rights entitlement (RE) introduced by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Sebi) earlier this year. The platform allows eligible shareholders to renunciate their shares for a price.
